> [!example] Maple's Vault Notation
> *Lucky Dice* -  **FL** / cc -> m!UF .JF1**J**M
> *Cat of Two Tails* -  **FL** / wc -> m!UF .JF1**J**M
> *Igneous Cross* -  **FL** / cc -> m!UF .JF1**J**M
>
> where ! is "walk around the structure in an anti-clockwise direction if right hand dominant"
***

A setup to bring two flicked structures into the sky and mount them.

**The key to these moves are to do them SLOWLY. You actually need the top structure to be falling back down before you jump and flick it.**
